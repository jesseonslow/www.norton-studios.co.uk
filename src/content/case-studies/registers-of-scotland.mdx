# Registers of Scotland

Registers of Scotland (RoS) is the non-ministerial government department responsible for compiling and maintaining 20 public registers. These registers relate to land, property, and other legal documents in Scotland. RoS is also responsible for the registration of marriages, civil partnerships, and deaths in Scotland.

## Case study

- Coaching teams on CI/CD, trunk based development, and Agile software delivery
- Defining the standards and best practices for the development team
- Developed internal tooling to increase developer productivity using node.js/TypeScript and Grafana
- Developing GitLab CI/CD pipelines to integrate AWS CDK deployment, SonarQube, OSV Security Scanning and automated testing

- Node.js
